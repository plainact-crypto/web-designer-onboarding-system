Project overview

  • Business name and website URL, if any.
  • Project type: new site, redesign, landing page, ecommerce, portfolio, or migration.
  • Primary goal of the website.
  • Secondary goals and success metrics.
  • Key audience and customer segments.

Page and content requirements

  • List every required page.
  • Define each page objective.
  • Assign content responsibility for each page.
  • Mark content status as final, draft, missing, or needs copywriting.
  • List media assets needed for each page.

Feature and integration requirements

  • Forms and where submissions should go.
  • Email marketing or CRM integrations.
  • Payment, booking, calendar, or ecommerce needs.
  • SEO basics such as titles, descriptions, redirects, and sitemap.
  • Analytics, tracking, and reporting requirements.

Operational requirements

  • Domain, DNS, hosting, and CMS access.
  • Maintenance expectations.
  • Training or handoff needs.
  • Approval process and stakeholders.
  • Timeline, milestones, and dependencies.
