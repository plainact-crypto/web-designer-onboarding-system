Brand assets

  • Logo files in vector and PNG formats.
  • Brand colors and font names.
  • Brand guidelines, if available.
  • Icons, illustrations, or design assets.
  • Approved photography style or reference examples.

Website content

  • Final page copy.
  • Product or service descriptions.
  • Team bios and headshots.
  • Testimonials, case studies, awards, and trust badges.
  • FAQ content and contact information.

Technical access

  • Current website admin access.
  • Domain registrar login.
  • Hosting control panel login.
  • DNS provider access, if separate.
  • Analytics, Search Console, email platform, CRM, and integration access.

Launch and legal materials

  • Privacy policy and terms provided by the client.
  • Cookie notice requirements.
  • Payment or ecommerce setup details.
  • Final approval contact.
  • Post-launch maintenance preferences.
